The base game released [on Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/997010/Police_Simulator_Patrol_Officers/) in November 2022. Players step into the role of a police officer. The game allows 2-player coop and was developed using [Unreal Engine](https://www.unrealengine.com/).<br>
The game has gotten two major add-ons: 
- [Highway Expansion](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2350410/Police_Simulator_Patrol_Officers_Highway_Patrol_Expansion/) - released in June 2024 
- [Contraband Expansion](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3768320/Police_Simulator_Patrol_Officers_Contraband_Expansion/) - released in November 2025

 I joined [Aesir Interactive](https://aesir-interactive.com/) in November 2023 and was part of the team working on live updates for the base game as well as the Contraband Expansion.

My tasks were not limited to, but included:
- Programming:
    - Ambulance using stretcher to collect heavily injured NPCs
    - Vehicle gadgets: Snare Net + Launch Control
    - Vehicle customization in garage
    - Felony Stop & Self-defense feature
    - Find hidden packages with Cargo Scanner player tool
- Leadership:
    - Overseeing development of the Contraband Expansion as tech-lead (~1 year)
    - Task allocation, planning, risk mitigation, bug triages, ...
